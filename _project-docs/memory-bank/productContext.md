# Product Context — Vestigia

## Why It Exists
Diabetic Peripheral Neuropathy affects ~50% of diabetic patients and often goes undetected until severe. Traditional detection methods require specialist equipment or subjective assessments. Vestigia uses thermal imaging to provide objective, non-invasive DPN screening at the point of care.

## Problems It Solves
1. **Early detection** — Thermal asymmetry in feet is an early DPN indicator before symptoms appear
2. **Clinic workflow** — Structured session management, device pairing, and history tracking in one app
3. **Patient access** — Patients can view their own results and track progression over time
4. **Clinical reporting** — Angiosome-level temperature analysis gives clinicians actionable data

## Target Users
- **Diabetes clinics / podiatry practices** — Primary operators
- **Diabetic patients** — Secondary consumers of results
- **Platform admins** — Manage users, clinics, and AI model configuration

## Key Differentiators
- Angiosome mapping (6 vascular zones per foot analyzed separately)
- TCI (Thermal Circulation Index) display
- Confidence scoring on AI classification
- Session history with trend tracking
